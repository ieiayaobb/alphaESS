Cooperation Partners

  • The Green Power Company

    The Green Power Company, established REC in 2001, and has grown to become technical specialists in provision of renewable energy, power storage, hot water, wind, electrical and plumbing services. As a diversified company, we specialize in providing sustainable solutions for government, commercial and domestic sectors. Technically astute, with a loyal employee base and striving for excellence in our allocated field of work, we have steadily earned a reputation for leadership, quality and ethical practice, which has led to strategic business partnerships with leading global brands.
    http://www.greenpowerco.com.au/

  • Off Grid Power Solutions

    Off Grid Power Solutions has been working in the field of battery storage and solar installations for 30 years with a family history of off grid installations going back to 1970.

    OGPS and its group partners Energy Wise Group, Power Factor & Voltage Optimisation Australia and Commercial Solar Group have trained Electrical technicians that service all power management and storage solutions nationally and through Australasia, with over 26 Registered and certified Off Grid Storage Solar Installers and 24 hour emergency service we are the team with experience.

    A family company that puts its name to every product and service it provides, OGPS is proud to be a provider of Alpha-ESS battery storage solutions.

    http://www.offgridpowersolutions.com.au
  • Battery Management System (BMS) of AlphaESS is developed on battery cell produced by one of the biggest lithium battery manufacturers in China, Tianjin Lishen Battery Joint-stock Co.,Ltd. This supplier boasts state-of-the-art production lines which ensure the uniformity and stable quality of battery cells. With the strong support of LISHEN, AlphaESS is able to produce high quality battery pack and offers end users the world-class product with competitive performance.
  • Owning combined expertise of experienced experts from solar energy, energy storage and the commitment of dynamic, powerful team, AX Power Solution Ltd. pushes forward economic use of renewable energies. With close collaboration with AX Power, AlphaESS is able to distribute products and reliable service to end users in European countries, particularly in Germany.
  • GoodWe (Jiangsu) Power Supply Technology Co., Ltd. has developed and produced seven series solar inverters. The maximum conversion efficiency reaches up to 98.8% with MPPT efficiency over 99.5% and THDi less than 1%,which achieve the world leading level. Cooperating with GoodWe, AlphaESS successfully implements and integrates hybrid inverter in both on-grid and off-grid PV storage systems.
  • Global Mainstream Dynamic Energy Technology Ltd. (GMDE) mainly focuses on the development, manufacturing and sales of high-efficient, cost-effective and differential energy storage inverters. Cooperating with GMDE, AlphaESS successfully extends hybrid inverter implementation in PV storage systems from one phase to three phases.
  • Solar360 Pty Ltd (Solar360) has been established to address a major gap in the wholesale solar equipment market in Australia. They source wide range of solar electrical products from wholesalers and manufacturers from China, Taiwan and Germany. With close collaboration with Solar360, AlphaESS is able to distribute products and reliable service to end users in Australia.
