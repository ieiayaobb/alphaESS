Career

International Sales

Job Requirements:
1. Bachelor degree or above, major in Sales Marketing. International Trade or related;
2. Fluent in English,second foreign languages preferred;
3. Familiar with MS-Office (Word, Excel, Outlook, PowerPoint);
4. Good communication, coordination and teamwork skill with good sense of service;
5. Working experience in PV or lithium battery industry is preferred.
Responsibilities:
1. In charge of sales in the responsible territories;
2. Fulfill the sales and payment plan;
3. Take care of the customer claims in the responsible territories.

Product Marketing Engineer

Job Requirements:
1. Bachelor degree or above in Electronic, Electrical Engineering, Automation,Computer or relevant majors,above 1 year working experiences;
2. Experiences in the photovoltaic, lithium battery, inverter industry or related markets is preferred;
3. Excellent technical documents written ability;
4. Responsibility, good communication and coordination skills.
Responsibilities:
1.Responsible for the new products analyze and definition;
2. Provide technical support of the products to the sales team;
3. In charge of create and maintain the all marketing documents of the products;
4. Provide the product strategy for the management team.

After Sales Service Engineer

Job Requirements:
1. Bachelor degree or above of Electronic, Electrical Engineering, Automation, Computer or other related major;
2. At least 3 or more years work experience, be familiar with international standard and technical specifications of the energy storage products;
3. Good team cooperation spirit and interpersonal communication skills;
4. Fluent in English-Both spoken and written，German is preferred;
5. Adapted to travel frequently.
Responsibilities:
1. Provide technical support to the clients of the products (lithium battery system and PV storage system), such as installation, debugging.
2. Provide product trainings to the local distributors and installers;
3. In charge of local after-sales warehouse management.

Intern

Job Requirements:
1. College degree or above, no major limited;
2. Good command of written and oral English (with CET_4 or above);
3. Familiar with Microsoft office;
4. Excellent study abilities, good communication coordination skills and team work spirit.
