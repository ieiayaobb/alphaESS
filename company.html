Company Introduction

Founded in 2012, Alpha-ESS specializes in providing advanced energy storage products and intelligent energy management solutions to the world. As an international high-tech company, Alpha’s business has expanded to Europe, Australia and all over the world. Most of our team members have overseas background, with years of experience in new energy and lithium battery industry.

"Smarten Your Energy" is our vision. Guided by this, Alpha-ESS colleagues, since the establishment of our company, have always dedicated to helping customers manage their own energy generation, storage, and optimization so as to reduce energy consumption, reduce energy costs, improve electrical safety, and ultimately achieve a self-sufficient green life.

At Intersolar Europe, the world’s most influential solar exhibition held in Munich this June, Alpha-ESS entered top 10 finalists of EES Award as the only Chinese enterprise thanks to energy storage system. Meanwhile, Alpha-ESS signed an insurance agreement framework with Mannheim, the biggest insurance company in Germany PV industry, and became the first company to do so in energy storage industry.

We firmly believe that energy network now is embracing the same chance as Internet did 15 years ago. Household energy storage is not only the last one kilometer of building an energy network to connect millions of households, but also the threshold for users to know and to further experience energy network. Thus starting from energy storage system, Alpha-ESS is pursuing an ambiguous goal to transform from a product manufacturer into a network service provider.
