Company Introduction

Alpha Energy Storage Solutions (AphaESS) is a fast growing Hi-tech company aiming to deliver a future fully powered by renewable energy.

The main product - STORION series, which integrates smart Energy Management System (EMS), hybrid inverter and LiFePO4 battery modules together, helps households and small businesses to maximize their energy flexibility generated by solar PV. After three-year R&D and field tests, the second generation products have become mature and are now distributing through well-established channels mainly in Europe and Asia-Pacific region.

These products have been highly recognized by the market. At recent Intersolar Europe Exhibition 2015, STORION series received finalist nomination for EES AWARD.  

Pursuing an ambitious goal to contribute to an intelligent energy network, AlphaESS is now developing smart home devices which can be controlled by EMS to provide customers a whole Smart Energy Solution.
