ENERGY
Morning
The energy produced by the PV module is used to optimize self-consuption;the excess energy is used to recharge the batteries.
Afternoon
When the batteries are fully charged and Alpha system already meets your self-consumption requirements, the redundant energy is fed into the pubulic grid.
Evening
Once the sun has set, Alpha system automatically switches to energy from batteries. If the battery capacity or power is insuffcient to meet your consumption requirements, electricity is then obtained from the public grid.
UPS
When the grid fails, Alpha system can switch to the off-grid mode automatically and supply the loads.
