New office of Alpha Suzhou has been put into use on May 20th, 2015.

Post At 04-19 03:03:03

On May 20th, 2015, Alpha Suzhou moved into a new office, which is situated in NW-02, No. 99, Jinji Lake Avenue, Suzhou Industrial park and occupies an area of over 650m2, twice bigger than the original office. Altogether there are five main parts in new office--- marketing and sales section, R&D section, test room, meeting room and show room. Green has been used as main colour in the new office to represent green energy and a great show room has been decorated to present our company history and product series. Right after the relocation of Suzhou office, Nantong office will also move to new buildings at end of July.
